About

What is BudgetBuddy?

BudgetBuddy is a simple budgeting app that helps you keep track of your expenses and income.

It is simple to get started! Just enter your name, email address and password to create an account. After you have registered, you can add a savings goal and set up your weekly, monthly and yearly budgets. You can also add any income or expenditures you have, and label them so that you can easily keep track.

Why did we create it?

We created BudgetBuddy to simple the expense and income tracking process. There are tools out there that make something as simple (and important!) as your finances so difficult. BudgetBuddy gives you a straightforward way to keep you on track to your savings goals.
